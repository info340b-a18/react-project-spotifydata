Spotify Web App Proposal

Topic Overview

The question of interest here is about the overall listening habits of users on spotify. People use spotify as their main music source every single day and listen to thousands of different songs, but currently there is not a clear way to see data about the music you listen to. I would like to create an application that synthesizes data about the listening habits of users, and then provides a summary detailing their music listening habits over time. The summary will include most listened to genres, songs, time of day of listening, most frequent artists, etc. Synthesizing all of this data will be possible because the API is extremely versatile and allows for a vast range of capabilities. I am looking forward to seeing how my own music listening is broken down.

Potential Applications

  • Analyzing Music habits, genres. time of listening, etc.
  • Another potential application idea that could stem from this would be the creation of curated playlists based on listening habits. Though spotify already provides something similar it often does not actually appeal to the music tastes of the consumer, and could be much improved. This process is something that is difficult to carry out, but the algorithm could hopefully look at all genres listened to and create curated playlists that represent the broadness of music tastes people listen to.
